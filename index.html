Who We Are

Empowering students to innovate in rocket science.

NKU Rocket Team provides students hands-on engineering experience in design, manufacturing, testing, and launching.

We emphasize a first-principles mindset, designing all rockets and liquid engines from scratch. By uniting diverse talents from around the university, we are developing the future of the aerospace industry right here in Northern Kentucky.

How WE reach for the stars.

Solid Rocketry Jan 2026 Launch

Star Catcher

NKU Rocket Team’s first solid rocketry project. Designed to reach over 5,000 ft of altitude, Star Catcher serves as our foundational flight platform.

Liquids Historical Milestone

Liquid Rocket Engine

The first student-built liquid rocket engine in the history of the Cincinnati area. A major leap in propulsion technology for our region.
